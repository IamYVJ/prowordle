How to Play

Guess the hidden word before you run out of tries.

  • Each guess must be a real word of the chosen length.
  • After every guess, the tiles change color to show how close you were.
W E A R Y

W is in the word and in the correct spot.

P I L L S

I is in the word but in the wrong spot.

V A G U E

U is not in the word at all.

Difficulty. On Medium and Hard, every revealed hint must be reused in your next guesses. Hard also mixes in rarer words.

Daily Challenge. Everyone gets the same word each day — come back tomorrow for a new one.
